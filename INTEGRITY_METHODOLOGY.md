# Integrity Score Formula (v1.0)

Base Score: 100

- Trade Frequency (-5 pts/trade): Excessive trading while in office.
- Sector Conflict (-15 pts/match): Owning stocks in industries you regulate via committees.
- Late Disclosure (-1 pt/10 days): Failure to report trades within the 30-day legal window.
- Bill Proximity (-20 pts/bill): Sponsoring legislation that impacts personal holdings.
